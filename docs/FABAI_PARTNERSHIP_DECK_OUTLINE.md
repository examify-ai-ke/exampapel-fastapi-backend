## Presentation Tips

1. **Keep it concise:** Aim for 15-20 minutes presentation + 10 minutes Q&A

2. **Lead with impact:** Start with the problem and your unique solution

3. **Show, don't just tell:** Use screenshots, demos, and real examples

4. **Emphasize alignment:** Constantly tie back to FabAI's mission

5. **Be data-driven:** Use metrics and evidence wherever possible

6. **Address concerns proactively:** Acknowledge challenges and your mitigation strategies

7. **End with clear next steps:** Make it easy for them to say yes

8. **Prepare for questions:** Anticipate technical, business, and partnership questions
