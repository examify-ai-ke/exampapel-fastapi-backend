## About ExamPapel

ExamPapel is a dual-sided EdTech ecosystem addressing critical challenges in academic assessment:

**For Students (B2C - Free Platform):**
- Digital access to authentic past examination papers from universities across Kenya and beyond

- Topic-based custom quiz generation for targeted practice

**For Institutions (B2B - Enterprise SaaS):**
- RAG-powered (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) exam paper creation using institutions' proprietary historical data
- Hallucination-free AI that operates strictly within institutional syllabi and past papers
- Human-in-the-loop workflow ensuring educators maintain full control and academic integrity
- Advanced analytics for curriculum alignment, difficulty benchmarking, and accreditation reporting
