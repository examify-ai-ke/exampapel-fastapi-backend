## 💡 Key Success Factors

### 1. **Personalization is Critical**
- Generic emails get ignored
- Reference specific FabAI initiatives
- Show you've done your homework
- Address specific pain points they've mentioned

### 2. **Lead with Value**
- What's in it for them?
- Don't just ask for help
- Frame as mutual opportunity
- Emphasize their benefits first

### 3. **Be Specific**
- Vague proposals go nowhere
- Propose concrete pilot program
- Define clear metrics and timeline
- Make it easy to say yes

### 4. **Show, Don't Tell**
- Demo your platform
- Share real examples
- Use data and metrics
- Provide testimonials

### 5. **Follow Up Persistently (But Politely)**
- Most deals happen after multiple touches
- Don't give up after one email
- Vary your approach (email, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Add value with each follow-up

## 📞 Sample Opening Lines for Calls

If you get a call, start strong:

**Option 1 (Problem-Focused):**
"Thanks for taking the time. I know educators are spending 20+ hours creating each exam paper, and institutions are hesitant to adopt AI because of hallucination concerns. We've built a solution that addresses both..."

**Option 2 (Alignment-Focused):**
"I've been following FabAI's work on quality assurance for AI in education, and I'm excited to share how ExamPapel embodies those principles in a working platform with real institutional partnerships..."

**Option 3 (Value-Focused):**
"I wanted to explore how ExamPapel could serve as a pilot platform for FabAI's quality assurance frameworks. We have the technical infrastructure, institutional partnerships, and commitment to safe AI that could make this a valuable collaboration..."

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:** Send generic, copy-paste emails  
✅ **Do:** Personalize with specific FabAI references

❌ **Don't:** Focus only on what you need  
✅ **Do:** Lead with what you offer them

❌ **Don't:** Be vague about next steps  
✅ **Do:** Propose specific actions and timeline

❌ **Don't:** Give up after one email  
✅ **Do:** Follow up persistently but politely

❌ **Don't:** Oversell or exaggerate  
✅ **Do:** Be honest about your stage and traction

❌ **Don't:** Send long, rambling emails  
✅ **Do:** Be concise and scannable

❌ **Don't:** Forget to proofread  
✅ **Do:** Check for typos and clarity
